About Dataset

This data was collected from Open Government Data Platform: data.gov.in which happens to be the website home to many other data sets that are linked to the government of India.
This Dataset contains 846,672 rows and 6 columns and contains the data of prices from 1997 to 2015.
The 6 columns are:
 • Date
 • Centre or city
 • Commodity
 • Price per kg
 • Region or state
 • Country


Data: Indian key Commodities Retail Prices
